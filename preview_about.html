About Us

Our Legacy of Glass Craftsmanship

Our Story

Shivhare Bangle Store has been a cornerstone in the wholesale bangle market of Firozabad, the city of bangles. For decades, we have been dedicated to preserving the traditional art of glass bangle making while innovating with modern designs and techniques.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to provide high-quality, durable, and beautiful glass bangles to retailers and wholesalers across India. Today, we are proud to be one of the trusted names in the industry.

Why Choose Us?

  • Authenticity: 100% genuine Firozabad glass.
  • Variety: An extensive catalog ranging from bridal sets to daily wear.
  • Wholesale Focus: Competitive pricing structures designed for business growth.
  • Reliability: Timely delivery and secure packaging for bulk orders.

Meet the Owner

Under the visionary leadership of our founder, we have expanded our reach and maintained strong relationships with our partners. We believe in transparency and mutual growth.
